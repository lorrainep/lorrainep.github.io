About Me

My life story? My journey has been a long one. BUT I've learned a lot. Who hasn't? I've learned that I have passion when it comes to helping people. I'm guessing it comes from being raised by the kindest mother. I want to help people. Throughout my life I've tinkered around with coding. I took several online courses to be introduced to the basics.

Most of us like to kick back and relax. Right? Well, there was a particular day where I was relaxing and I started day dreaming about being able to develop what my mind could create. I started thinking about the video games I play. I started combining all sorts of different ideas when it came to VR. I decided during the first day of day dreaming about creating whats in my head that I wanted to learn to code.

Learning is power. When I say POWER, I think about KNOWLEDGE. Nerds rule the world am I right?
